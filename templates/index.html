introduction

The Atlantis Universe is a universe inspired by the legendary Atlantis civilization.

11,000 years ago, Atlantis was a civilization of significant technological progress, becoming the most prosperous continent on Earth. Due to its arrogance, Atlantis angered the gods and suffered devastating punishment. Zeus and other Olympian Gods sank the entire continent to the bottom of the sea, civilization collapsed. Most of the inhabitants of Atlantis perished; the few who survived were either half-human, half-god, or monster.

These demigod descendants, thanks to their unique abilities, have adapted to life on the ocean floor. They are determined to restore and develop the destroyed civilization. This journey is full of hardships, with countless dangerous challenges. Play as the demigods of Atlantis to begin the journey to restore the lost civilization.

business vision

The Atlantis Universe is a pioneer blockchain-based metaverse project. With the mission of making this project a journey of emotional, spatial, and valuable connections, Atlantis is more than just a fantasy-themed strategy game with a deep setting and storyline.

As we want Atlantis to utilize both Play-2-Earn and Play-4-Fun features, players in Atlantis Metaverse are the actual creators of the world, not simply participants.

Characters

Descendants of Poseidon
Class: Fighter

Humanoid species that possess incredible physical strength. With Poseidon's blood running through their veins, these champions always determine to rule the entire ocean and other species.

Descendants of Phorcys
Class: Guardian

A mermaid Species, they are the descendants of Phorcys, the ancient God who ruled the sea before Poseidon. Thus, they do not get along well with the descendants of Poseidon because they think Poseidon himself overthrew Phorcys.

Descendants of Triton
Class: Fighter

Half-man, half-octopus, like the descendants of Poseidon, This Species is blessed with immense physical strength. They have quite close relationships with The Descendants of Poseidon

Descendants of Oceanid the Sea Fairy
Class: Priest

A mermaid Species, unlike other Descendants, this species is particularly peaceful. They have healing spells that can treat serious wounds as well as revive lives. The Descendants of Oceanids are loved and praised by the people of Atlantis.

Descendants of Graeae the Sea Witch
Class: Mage

A humanoid species, they wield outstanding power. But because of arrogance as well as the overuse of dark arts, they have constantly caused many explosions. This species is not friendly, so they do not have good relationships with other Species.

Descendants of Thaumas
Class: Assassin

A humanoid species, they are the master of disguise; they also have incredible speed and reflexes. They have a very close-knit relationship with the Descendants of Oceanids, because, in the past of their ancestors, Thaumas and Oceanid are husband and wife.

Descendants of Gorgon
Class: Mage

A humanoid species, with the ability to use overwhelming magic power. They always desire to dominate the seas so they formed alliances with the Descendants of Phorcys.

GAME PLAY

Main feature

Entertainment

Players will be able to engage in various types of gameplay such as Tournaments, Quests, Raid bosses, et. We are also happy to keep the game updated on a particular period basis to allow players to participate in more features later.

Ownership

With the unique feature of NFT, characters, items, and equipment will be offered for the first time in limited quantities. Players not only gain a sense of achievement but are also the real owners of The Atlantis Universe. Also, soon, DAO will be developed and perfected to help with user asset management.

Connection between digital assets and physical assets

The Atlantis Universe will develop a real estate NFT platform, where players can buy virtual real estate or actual, tangible real estate. Actual real estate and the benefits contained in the game can also be converted into physical real estate.

Personalization and user experience

The game also provides a customization experience where players can request alternation of equipment, characters, and items. All user assets will be guaranteed to be unique in appearances and effects to create a rich, colorful and diverse universe here in Atlantis.
