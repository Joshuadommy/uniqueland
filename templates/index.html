Game Concept

The universe of virtual land is covered by different creatures, one of them are called “Karraban”,in which are famous for being strong. The Karraban live on planet Neptune where there are a lot of forgotten artefacts from the lost kingdom

Korriban, however, is incomplete and only show themselves to those humans who they find worthy of validating their existence. In return for this companionship, they offer humans their incredible powers.

During sleep, Karraban is vulnerable and prefer to stay securely inside their human’s wallet. They are always on the lookout for strong and worthy humans and are on a constant quest to better themselves through combat and duels with other trainers.
